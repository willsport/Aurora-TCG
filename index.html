About Aurora TCG Australia

A Queensland-based hobby retailer focused on sealed product, long-term relationships and reliable fulfilment.

Who we are

Aurora TCG Australia was created to bring high-quality trading card products to collectors, families and competitive players across the country. Our focus is on sealed product – booster boxes, cases, ETBs and accessories – sourced through authorised distributors.

All orders are packed and shipped from Queensland with care to protect sealed box integrity for collectors and investors.

How we operate

We commit to clear preorder communication, fast dispatch on in-stock items and transparent stock levels that reflect confirmed allocations. As the business grows, Aurora TCG plans to support local events and community play, building a stable ecosystem for long-term TCG growth in Australia.

For distributors and partners, we maintain consistent ordering patterns, organised storage and professional fulfilment processes.

Key Store Policies

Clear policies give confidence to customers and suppliers. These can be expanded into full standalone pages if needed.

Preorder Policy

Aurora TCG only accepts preorders for products we are confirmed to receive via authorised distributors. All preorder quantities are tied to official allocations. If allocations change, impacted customers are contacted promptly and offered a refund or suitable alternative.

Shipping & Handling

Orders are typically dispatched within 1–2 business days of payment clearing. All shipments include tracking as standard. Booster boxes and cases are shipped double-boxed with adequate padding to protect corners and factory seal.

Returns & Refunds

Sealed products must remain unopened to be eligible for change-of-mind returns. Faulty or damaged-on-arrival items are handled in line with Australian Consumer Law. Customers should contact us within 7 days of delivery if there is an issue.
